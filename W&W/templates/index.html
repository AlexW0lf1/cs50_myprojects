Welcome to Wands and Wizards app v.0!

Introduction

If you are a fan of both Dungeons & Dragons and Harry Potter, we present to you Wands & Wizards, a Harry Potter 5e Adaptation created by Murphen44. Joining the magic of the Wizarding World with the gameplay of D&D 5e, this comprehensive Wizarding Alternative for Novelty Dungeoneering Stories (or W.A.N.D.S.) Rulebook substitutes 5e races, classes, subclasses, backgrounds, feats, skills, equipment, and spells. Like the 5e PHB, Part 1 of the W.A.N.D.S. Rulebook contains new character creation options. Part 2 focuses on the changes made to spellcasting and all of the new spells from the Harry Potter series. If you are unfamiliar with any of the Harry Potter references, please review the Glossary at the end of the W.A.N.D.S. Rulebook.

How to play

You can create a new session following Your sessions, or join any existing one at Join. Be sure to check Rulebook, here or at Murphen44 page, to learn basic game features. Ratings are not implemented in v.0.

When creating new session, you can add players to it using their usernames. If you don't know any yet - don't worry, Your session will appear at Join and anyone would be able to send you request to join your session. Once you created your session, you become a GM (Game Master), which means that you lead the session. Session will appear at Your sessions -> GM sessions. When you have at least 2 players in your session, you might start session. Players will get notifications that session is started.

If you joined session as player, you need to create your character. Find session at Sessions -> Player sessions and you will see list of sessions you joined as player. To create your character, use "Create character" button.

Once you were redirected to Create character page, you will see bunch of parameters to choose, You can read Rulebook For better understanding. However, once you chosen one of the options, you will see description with the most important information about it. You can collaborate with your GM on your character creation!

There is no voice chat in W&W app v.0, so you and your party may use whatever you like, because W&W sessions require verbal communications (especially in v.0).

Implemented features v.0

Gm can level up characters, do damage or heal (all enemies and skills are not implemented, GM still needs to keep info about them on paper). Players will see all that events in log window, under cards with character's info. Damage and heal buttons affect health, Level up button affects level and health. Read rulebook to see formula(depends on character's Casting style).
